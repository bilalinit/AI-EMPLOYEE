# Stop Losing 10 Hours/Week to Tasks AI Can Handle

Here's the uncomfortable truth: Most business owners I talk to are spending 15-20 hours a week on administrative work that doesn't generate revenue.

Email triage. Scheduling. Invoice follow-ups. Social media drafting.

These tasks aren't bad—they're necessary. But they're not worth $150+/hour of your time.

**The automation mindset shift:**

Last month, I helped a client implement a simple AI system that:
• Auto-organized their inbox (saved 5 hrs/week)
• Drafted client responses for review (saved 3 hrs/week)
• Flagged urgent messages instantly (saved stress)

Result? They reclaimed an entire workday. Every single week.

**The key insight:** You don't need a $50k automation overhaul. Start small. Identify your 3 most repetitive tasks. Automate those first.

That's where the ROI lives.

What's the ONE task you'd automate first if you could?

## Key Takeaways
- Track your time for 1 week to identify repetitive tasks
- Start with email, scheduling, or social media drafting
- Use AI as a draft-and-review system (keeps you in control)
