# The Employee That Never Sleeps 🌙

Imagine having a team member who works 24/7, never takes breaks, doesn't call in sick, and handles repetitive tasks without complaint. Sounds too good to be true? Welcome to the era of Digital FTEs. 🤖

Digital Full-Time Equivalents are AI-powered employees that can handle customer inquiries, schedule appointments, process data, and even create content—all while you focus on what matters: growing your business.

The future isn't about replacing humans. It's about freeing them to do what they do best: strategize, create, and connect.

✅ Consistent quality and output

✅ Handles repetitive tasks automatically
