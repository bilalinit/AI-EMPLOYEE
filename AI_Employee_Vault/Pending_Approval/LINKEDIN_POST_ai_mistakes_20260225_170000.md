# 3 AI Mistakes That Waste Money (And What to Do Instead)

Everyone's talking about AI. But most businesses I see are doing it wrong—and burning cash in the process.

Here are the 3 most expensive mistakes:

**Mistake #1: Automating Before Optimizing**

You can't AI your way out of a broken process. I see businesses throwing AI at workflows that shouldn't exist in the first place.

*Fix*: Map your process first. Cut the unnecessary steps. THEN automate what's left.

**Mistake #2: The "Everything at Once" Trap**

Trying to automate your entire business overnight leads to half-baked systems, frustrated teams, and zero ROI.

*Fix*: Start with ONE high-impact task. Nail it. Expand from there. We recommend starting with email triage, scheduling, or customer inquiries—pick one.

**Mistake #3: Set It and Forget It**

AI isn't magic. It needs monitoring, refinement, and human oversight. The businesses getting results? They're constantly tweaking and improving.

*Fix*: Treat AI like a new employee. Train it. Review its work. Improve over time.

The companies winning with AI aren't the ones with the biggest budgets—they're the ones who implement strategically, iterate constantly, and focus on ONE thing at a time.

## Key Takeaways
- Optimize processes before automating them
- Start with ONE high-impact task, not everything
- Treat AI like an employee—monitor, train, improve
