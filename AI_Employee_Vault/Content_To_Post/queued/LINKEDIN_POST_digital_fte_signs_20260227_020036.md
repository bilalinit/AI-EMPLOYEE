# 3 Signs Your Business Needs a Digital FTE (And You Might Not Even Know It)

You're drowning in busy work. You know you need help, but hiring another full-time employee feels overwhelming — the cost, the training, the management overhead.

What if you could hire someone who works 24/7, never calls in sick, and handles the repetitive tasks that drain your energy?

Enter the **Digital FTE** — an AI-powered Full-Time Equivalent that transforms how businesses operate.

### Here are 3 signs you're ready:

**1. You're spending more time on admin than revenue-generating work**
If invoice processing, email responses, and data entry are eating up your day, a Digital FTE can reclaim 10-20 hours weekly. Imagine what you could do with that time.

**2. You're scaling but your systems aren't**
Growth is great until it breaks your processes. Digital FTEs scale instantly — no hiring delays, no training curves. They handle increased workload without breaking a sweat.

**3. You're losing leads to slow response times**
Customers expect fast responses. A Digital FTE monitors inquiries 24/7, qualifies leads, and ensures nothing falls through the cracks — even while you sleep.

The future of work isn't replacing humans. It's amplifying them with intelligent automation that handles the mundane so you can focus on what matters.

## Key Takeaways
- Digital FTEs work 24/7 without breaks or burnout
- They scale instantly with your business needs
- You reclaim 10-20 hours weekly for high-value work
