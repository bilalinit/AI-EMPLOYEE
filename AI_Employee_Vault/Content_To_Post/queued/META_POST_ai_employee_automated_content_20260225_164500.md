# Just Built an AI Employee That Never Sleeps 🤖💤

While most people were sleeping last night, my AI employee was working.

Here's what I just built:

**An AI-powered content creator that:**
✅ Generates LinkedIn posts automatically
✅ Runs on a schedule (every day at 2 AM)
✅ Creates fresh content based on my business goals
✅ Writes in my brand voice
✅ Queues posts for my review before posting

**How it works:**
1. Cron job triggers at 2:00 AM daily
2. Calls Claude Code with the /linkedin-posting skill
3. Claude reads my business goals and audience
4. Generates a unique, engaging post
5. Saves it to Pending Approval for my review
6. I review, approve, and it posts automatically

The result? I wake up to fresh content ready to publish. Every. Single. Day.

This is what a Digital FTE looks like in 2026. Not replacing humans—amplifying them.

## Key Takeaways
- AI can handle repetitive content creation while you sleep
- Automation = consistency (the secret to social media growth)
- Your Digital FTE works 24/7, even at 2 AM
