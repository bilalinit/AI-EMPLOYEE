# The Best Creators in 2025 Aren't Choosing Between AI and Human Creativity—They're Using Both

Here's a controversial take: AI isn't killing creativity. It's killing the *boring* parts of creativity.

Every day, I see businesses struggling with content production. They want stunning visuals, engaging videos, consistent social media presence—but they're bottlenecked by the time it takes to create.

**The new creative workflow looks like this:**

→ AI handles ideation, storyboarding, and first drafts
→ Humans inject emotion, brand voice, and strategic thinking
→ AI speeds up rendering and editing
→ Humans polish the final product with that "spark" only people have

Last week, we used AI to accelerate a 3D animation project from 30 days to under 7—without compromising quality. The AI handled the grunt work. Our artists focused on the creative decisions that actually matter.

**The businesses winning right now?**

They're not asking "AI or human?" They're asking "How do I combine both to create something neither could achieve alone?"

AI + Human creativity isn't just faster—it's better.
